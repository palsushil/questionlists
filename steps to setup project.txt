Please follow the steps to set-up projects.

1) Unzip the drupal_code.zip and drupal_DB.zip.
2) After unzip the drupal_code.zip file put folder to under the htdocs(for xxamp) and www(for wamp).
3) Create a database and import the drupal_DB unziped file.
4) Now set-up the setting.php (sites/default/setting.php)
5) Now you can access your site localhost/projectfolder_name
6) You can setup the virtual host for you site.
